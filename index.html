Origins of this project.

So I decided to learn CV and NLP durinng my last semester at the New School. Though it would be fun to learn something new.

The idea is to track someone down - in this case ISIS. Once I set up the scraper I will expand the project to looking for neo-nazis in Russia. The main idea is to track someone when you don't know whom you're looking for.

Besides just finding images that look like ISIS flags I want to look into their previous tweets and the tweet that contained an image. The goal here is to determine how to distinguish supporters from News media, critics and so on.
